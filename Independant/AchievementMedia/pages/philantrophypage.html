Philantrophy

Our team is dedicated to giving back to the community. We beleive in lending a hand and have worked with local non profits to make a positive impact. In the past we have donated $20,000 to various medical research foundations, and have participated as a team in walk for the cure campaigns

Grants to local High Schools

We provide education grants to local high schools. In 2017 we gave Gerald Hilman High School a grant that helped fund new books for thier library, furniture for thier classrooms and a college assistance program for first generation college students

Free Sandwiches to the Community

We beleive a strong community is important and every month we make over 200 sandwiches and hand them out for free to those in need along with snacks, chips, fruit, tea, coffee and other beverages.
